We are passionate Scottish Country Dancers!

Located in Portland, Oregon, we teach and dance the social dances of Scotland and promote socialization with nearby RSCDS branches.

Demo
Experienced dancers can be hired to come to your event to show you what Scottish country dancing looks like.
Class
Come to our weekly classes in Lake Oswego and we will teach you the steps, figures, and techniques to have a fun time.
Party
Test your new skills once a month when the branch holds its monthly dance party!
Dance
Once a year, dancers from all over the Pacific Northwest travel to Portland to foster inter-branch socialization and dances.
Travel
Your can take your dancing skills to RSCDS branches around the world and dance with them!
