Demonstrations

The Portland Branch has a demonstration team that performs at various Scottish events and also at area retirement homes on request.

Our Demonstration Team is available to dance at your function. We can perform dances and, if you would like, teach a few dances to those in attendance. Prices depend on location, performance length, and number of dancers involved. Ask for details.
