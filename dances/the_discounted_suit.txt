Name: The Discounted Suit
Format: 8×32R, 3C (4C set)
Source: D. Wong, The De’il Amang the Tailors
BARS
  1-8	1s+2s set & dance RH across ½ way, set & dance LH across ½ way back to place
 9-12	1s+2s lead down the middle
13-16	1s lead back to top while 2m cross 2w in front and lead up to between 3s
17-18	1s ½ turn downwards with both hands while setting, to end in 2nd place on opposite sides
19-20	1s arch, 2w dances under the arch to 1st place own side
21-22	1s ½ turn with both hands while setting
23-24	1s arch again, 2m dances under the arch to 1st place own side
25-32	2s+1s+3s circle 6H round & back
