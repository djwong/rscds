Name: Turkey Trot
Source: J. Hamilton, Let’s All Dance
Format: 8×32R, 3C (4C set)
BARS
 1- 8	1s lead down for 3 steps, back to top & cast to 2nd places
 9-16	1s dance RH across (Lady with 2s & Man with 3s) & LH across with other couple
17-24	2s+1s+3s Adv+Ret & dance DoSiDo
25-32	2s+1s+3s circle 6H round & back
