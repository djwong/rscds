Name: How Does it Go?
Source: Marjorie Adams, SCD Archives
Format: 8×32J, 3C (4C set)
BARS
1- 8	1s set and cast 1 place (2s step up), dance 1/2 Figure of 8 round 2s, end in 2nd place opposite sides, facing down
9-16	1s turn 3s (with nearer hand – 1s dancing in) into 1/2 Figure of 8 round 3s and end facing 1st corners
17-24	1s dance Hello-Goodbye setting and end back to back (Man facing up and Lady down)
25-32	1s dance Reels of 3 across (Man with 2s and Lady with 3s) giving RSh to 2nd corner, 1s end own side
