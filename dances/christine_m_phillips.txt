Name: Christine M. Phillips
Format: 8×32J, 3C (4C set)
Source: J. Brenchley, RSCDS Book 46
BARS
1- 8	1s set advancing and lead down below 3s crossing 1s cast to 2nd place opp sides and turn LH to face 1st corners
9-16	1s dance ½ reel of 4 with 1st corners, 1s turn 3rd corners (pstn) RH and turn LH to face 2nd corners
17-24	1s dance ½ reel of 4 with 2nd corners, 1s turn 4th corners (pstn) RH and turn LH to Man facing up Lady down
25-32	1M+3s (at top) also 1L+2s dance RH across, 1s turn LH to 2nd place own sides while 2s+3s chase ½ way clockwise
