Name: Winter Wonder
Format: 8×32R, 3C (4C set)
Source: J. Lattaille, Fun for All Seasons
BARS
1–8	1s cross RH, cast off (2s up), dance down btwn 3s cast up to 2nd place
9–16	1s set to and turn 2s RH 1/2, then 1s set to and turn 2s LH 1/2. Men face out
17-24	1s+2s dance Ladies' Chain.
25-32	2s+1s+3s adv & ret, 1s dance 1/2 Fig 8 round 3s
