Name: Tribute to the Borders
Format: 8×32J, 3C (4C set)
Source: R. Goldring, RSCDS Leaflet 11
Youtube: wPfMu6dQ8V4
BARS
1- 8	1s cross RH and cast 1 place, turn RH 1½ times to end in prom hold facing 2M
9-16	1s dance ½ RSh reel of 3 on Mans side, cross to face 3L and dance ½ RSh reel on Ladies side 1s end facing 3L (in top place)
17-24	1s set to 3L, turn and set to 2L, turn and set to 2M, turn and set to 3M ending with 3s+1s+2s in centre ready for Allemande
25-32	3s+1s+2s Allemande
