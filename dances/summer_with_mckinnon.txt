Name: Summer with McKinnon
Format: 8×32S, 3C (4C set)
Source: R. Downey, They Stole My Wife
BARS
1- 8	1s set, cast (2’s up), set, turn BH end backs to 1st corners
9-16	1s cast into Pass+Twirl with 1st corners, face ptnr then cast into Pass+Twirl with 2nd corners & end facing 1st corner person (who is in own third corner position)
17-24	1s set to and turn corners (on opposite sides) end on opposite sides L face diag up M down
25-32	1s dance Diag R&L (1M crossing down & 1L crossing up to start)
