Name: Hamish’s Trip to Inverary
Format: 8×32S, 3C (4C set)
Source: Tony Moretti, Hamish Dances
BARS
1- 8	1s & 2s dance RH across for 2 bars then cross over RH; 1s & 3s dance LH across for 2 bars then cross over LH
9-16	3s & 1s (bottom 2 couples) set and link; 2s & 1s (top 2 couples) set and link
17-24	1s & 2s dance the Knot, 1s end facing 1st corners
25-32	Hello-Goodbye setting, on bar 7 of the phrase, all clap as 1s petronella set to end 2nd place own sides
