Format: Two Facing Two; Around the Room Jig
BARS
1-8	All clap and dance four hands round and back.
9-16	Half rights and lefts. Start by crossing right with your opposite. Near hands joined with your opposite, dance out or into the circle, away from your partner, for two bars and back for two bars.
17-24	Half rights and lefts. Start by crossing right with your partner. Near hands joined with your opposite, dance out or into the circle, away from your partner, for two bars and back for two bars.
25-32	Take near hands with partner, set, advance , retire and pass right shoulders with that couple and on to a new couple.
