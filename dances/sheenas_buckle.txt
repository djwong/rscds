Name: Sheena’s Buckle
Format: 8×32J, 3C (4C set)
Source: Bill Zobel and Muriel A Johnstone, Merse Collection
BARS
1- 8	1s dance ½ Fig of 8 round 2s, 1s dance ½ Fig of 8 round 3s as 2s step up 
9-16	1s dance up to 2s (who face down), join hands and set, 1s ¾ turn 2s with nearer hand and 2s+1s set to partners 
17-24	2s+1s+3s circle 6H round and back 
25-32	1s+3s dance non-progressive Knot (usual Knot but end dancing ½ LH across)
