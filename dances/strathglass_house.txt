Name: Strathglass House 
Format: 8×32S, 3C (4C set)
Source: Robert Bremner, RSCDS Book 13 
Youtube: Z7gb3FAquZI
BARS
1-2	1s2s3s set;
3-4	1s2s3s 6 hands round to the left halfway;
5-8	repeat bars 1-4 to original places;
9-16	1s cross down below 2s, cast round 3s, lead to the top and cast to finish facing first corners;
17-24	1s turn corner, partner, corner, partner, finishing in 2nd place on opposite sides;
25-28	2s1s3s advance and retire;
29-32	1s turn both hands 1½ times.
