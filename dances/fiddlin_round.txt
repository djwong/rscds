Name: Fiddlin’ Round
Format: 8×32J, 3C (4C set)
Source: B. McMurtry, Devil’s Quandary
BARS
1- 8	1s cross RH & cast 1 place (2s step up), 1s dance ½ Fig of 8 up through the 2s.
 9-16	1s turn 1st corner RH, pass RSh in the middle, turn 2nd corner RH, pass RSh in the middle to 2nd place own side facing out.
17-24	1s going to their R, dance figure of 8 on own side.
25-32	2s+1s+3s circle 6H round & back.
