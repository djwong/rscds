Name: Those Eyes
Format: 8×32J
Source: Bruce Herbold
BARS
1-8	1L and 2M dance back to back; then loop in towards each other and pull back right shoulder to cast into neighbor’s place.  Partners step up/down on 7-8.
9-16	1M and 2L repeat.
17-24	1s and 2L circle to the left; 1M and 2L drop hands to allow 2M to join the circle and circle right, ending in center with partner.
25-32	Pousette.
