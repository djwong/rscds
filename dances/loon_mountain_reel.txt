Name: Loon Mountain Reel
Source: SCD for Children and Teens
Format: 4×32R, 3C (4C set)
BARS
1–8	Circle 8 hands round and back
9–16	All advance and retire twice
17–24	1c+2c & 3c+4c right hands across and left hands back
25–32	1c turn RH and face down, the others step in on bar 28 and form arches ; 1c lead under arches to 4pl, 2c+3c+4c retiring to sidelines one place up on bar 32. (2,3,4,1)
