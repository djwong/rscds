Name: The Magic of Merrill
Youtube: hmpTcDaGSbw#t=8
Source: L. Ramshaw Leaflet
Format: 8×32R 3C (4C set)
BARS
1- 8	1s+2s set and 1s cross down (no hands) to Double Triangle positions as 2s cast up, 2s+1s+3s set and 1s turn 1st corners 3⁄4 RH
9-16	1s dance 1⁄2 LSh reels of 3 across (1L with 2s, 1M with 3s) into RSh half reels of 3 on own sides and 1s pass LSh to face 1st corner positions
17-24	1s dance 1⁄2 reel of 4 with corners and pass RSh to face 2nd corner positions, 1s dance 1⁄2 reel of 4 with corners and end 2nd place own sides
25-32	2s+1s+3s circle 6H round and back
