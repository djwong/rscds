Name: The Lass of Richmond Hill
Format: 8×32R, 3C (4C set)
Source: F. Turnbull, Richmond Anniv. Book
Youtube: l0HwPhGJLGU
BARS
1- 8	1s lead down the middle and back to top
9-16	1s cast 1 place and cross passing LSh, cast round 1st corner and turn partner LH to face 1st corners
17-32	1s dance Corner Chain with 1st and 2nd corners:-
'	1s change pl RH with 1st corners, 1st corners turn LH in centre and return to places giving RH to 1s who turn LH in centre to face 2nd corners
'	1s change pl RH with 2nd corners, 2nd corners turn LH in centre and return to places giving RH to 1s and 1s end with a ½ turn LH to 2nd place own sides
