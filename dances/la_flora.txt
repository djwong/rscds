Name: La Flora
Format: 8×32J, 3C (4C set)
Source: Monro, RSCDS Book 27
BARS
1- 8	1s+2s dance RH across ½ way, ½ turn RH, dance LH across ½ way and turn LH to places 
9-16	1s petronella to 2nd place opposite sides 
17-24	2s+1s+3s circle 6H round and back 
25-32	1s dance reels of 3 on opposite sides giving RSh to person on their right and cross to 2nd place own sides
