BARS
1-8	1st and 2nd couples set, cross over giving right hands, set, and turn halfway with right hands to face up in promenade hold. 
9-16	1st and 2nd couples Allemande. 
17-24	1st couple dance mirror figures of eight on own sides. They dance in and up round 2nd couple to begin, and take hands briefly when they meet. 
25-32	All three couples circle six hands round and back.  Repeat having passed a couple.
