Monthly Dances

On the second Saturday of every month (except March and August), the Portland branch has a dance party at Tigard Grange. Though some months have prizes, some have giveaways, and others have live music, every month has dancing! For 2010-2011, the parties are (tentatively) scheduled for 10/9, 11/13, 12/11, 2/12, 4/9, and 5/14. Parties are not held in January, and the Ball is held in March.
