Monthly Dances

On the second Saturday of every month (except March and August), the Portland branch has a dance party at Tigard Grange. Though some months have prizes, some have giveaways, and others have live music, every month has dancing! For 2008, the parties are (tentatively) scheduled for 1/12, 2/9, 4/12, 5/10, 6/14(?), 10/11, 11/8 and 12/13.
