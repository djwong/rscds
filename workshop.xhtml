Workshop and Ball

Every year, the Portland group puts on a dancing workshop and a ball some time in March. The 34th ball is March 9th, 2013 at the Christ Episcopal Church in Lake Oswego, Ore. Check back for more details as they are available! Like us on Facebook!

Time

9:30am for the workshop; lunch from noon to 1:15pm; classes end at 4:00pm; 7:00pm for the ball.

Location

Christ Episcopal Church
1060 Chandler Road.
Lake Oswego, OR 97034

Teachers

Lea and James Maiolo, Redmond Wash.
